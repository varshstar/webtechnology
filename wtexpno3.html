OUR SCHOOL

SR Matriculation Higher Secondary School

With a noble view to provide proper Matriculation Educational to various levels of Children in the Society, the school was founded in 2010 and it was upgraded as Matriculation School in 2012 and as Matriculation Higher Secondary School in 2014.

The School is located at a distance of 40km from Erode, 18km from Chennimalai and 15 km from Tiruppur. Our School is situated at a calm area in an extent of 6.0 acres at karatupalyam near Uthukuli. The School occupies a prominent place with unique characteristic of its own and providing Co-Education from Pre. K.G. to XII Std. there are 17 vehicles operating for a convenience of the students.
Good infrastructure is existing with all the required facilities. The classes are accommodated in 2 and 3 storied Terraced buildings. Separate Hostels are provided inside the Campus for Boys and Girls with entire facilities. Since enough facilities available to carry more than double of above strength in hostels a special attention is being given to students. A herd of 10 cows is being maintained to provide Pure Milk and Curd for the Hostel Students
