OUR HISTORY

With a noble view to provide proper Matriculation Educational to various levels of Children in the Society, the school was founded in 2010 and it was upgraded as Matriculation School in 2012 and as Matriculation Higher Secondary School in 2014.

OUR MISSION

The Schools motto being "LOVE, SERVICE, PURITY" the school strives to realize these principles in the tender and healthy minds of the students. Our objective is not only to impart Knowledge but also to develop a good Character with Personality.

OUR VISSION
